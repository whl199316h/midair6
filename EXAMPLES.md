Demo examples for ZulipVoting
====

*This will start a voting* - In the public stream
@VotingBot Karaoke
Go to X place
Go to Hopper room
Go to my home

*This would be a vote* - In a private message
@VotingBot Karaoke
1

*This will end a voting and get the results* - In the public stream
@VotingBot Karaoke
results
